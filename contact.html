Shipping & Delivery

We offer worldwide shipping with temperature-controlled packaging. Standard delivery takes 5-10 business days for international orders.

Storage Conditions

Our lyophilized reagents can be stored at room temperature (15-25°C) for 12+ months. Liquid reagents require 2-8°C refrigeration.

Certifications
