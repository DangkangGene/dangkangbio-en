About Dangkang Bio

Wuhan Dangkang Bio-Technology Co., Ltd. - Your Partner in Precision Bio-Detection

Company Overview

Wuhan Dangkang Bio-Technology Co., Ltd. is a high-tech enterprise headquartered in Optics Valley Bio-City, Wuhan East Lake High-Tech Development Zone. We are dedicated to the research, development, and application of isothermal nucleic acid amplification technology.

Our mission is to build precise, convenient and accessible bio-detection products that make advanced molecular diagnostics available to everyone. With a team of PhD and Master's level talents in biotechnology, we specialize in RPA-based rapid detection solutions.

We are currently expanding our presence to the Australian market, bringing our cutting-edge detection technology to biosecurity, food safety, and healthcare applications Down Under.

Technical Advantages

What sets Dangkang Bio apart in the biotech industry

Independent Raw Material Production

Unlike many competitors who rely on imported enzymes, we have established our own enzyme production capabilities. This ensures a stable supply chain, better quality control, and more competitive pricing.

  • Self-developed enzyme formulations
  • Consistent quality across batches
  • Reduced dependency on suppliers
  • Customizable enzyme properties

Proprietary Lyophilized Microsphere Technology

Our advanced freeze-drying technology produces stable microsphere reagents that can be stored at room temperature. This innovation eliminates cold chain requirements and extends shelf life significantly.

  • Room temperature storage (12+ months)
  • No cold chain shipping required
  • Pre-aliquoted for easy use
  • Excellent stability under varying conditions

Cost-Effective & Quality Controllable

Vertical integration from enzyme production to kit assembly allows us to offer competitive pricing while maintaining strict quality standards throughout the manufacturing process.

  • Full production chain control
  • Rigorous quality testing protocols
  • ISO-compliant manufacturing
  • Batch-to-batch consistency
