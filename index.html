Recombinase Polymerase Amplification (RPA) is a rapid nucleic acid amplification technology that achieves isothermal amplification at 37-42°C through the synergistic action of recombinase, single-stranded DNA binding protein, and strand-displacing DNA polymerase. Compared to traditional PCR, RPA offers faster reaction speed, higher sensitivity (detecting single-copy templates), and lower equipment dependency. It is widely applicable in pathogen detection, food safety, agriculture, and primary healthcare.

Dangkang Gene, with years of deep expertise in molecular detection, has complete independent development and production capability of RPA amplification enzyme systems, significantly reducing the cost of RPA raw enzyme materials while ensuring controllable product quality. Combined with optimized lyophilization processes, we further enhance transport convenience and stability. We have built a comprehensive product system covering RPA lyophilized enzymes, portable detection equipment, and full-range supporting reagents, providing one-stop solutions for scientific research innovation and on-site molecular rapid testing.
