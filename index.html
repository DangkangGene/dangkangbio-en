Core Technology Advantages

RPA technology delivers superior performance across all key detection parameters

Rapid Amplification

Achieves detectable product levels within 5-20 minutes, dramatically faster than traditional PCR methods that require 1-2 hours.

Accurate Results

High specificity and sensitivity comparable to PCR, with single-copy detection capability for ultimate precision.

Easy Operation

Minimal training required. Simple sample-to-answer workflow that can be performed by any laboratory technician.

Closed-tube detection format minimizes aerosol contamination that commonly affects conventional PCR workflows.

About Dangkang Bio

Wuhan Dangkang Bio-Technology Co., Ltd. is a high-tech enterprise dedicated to the development and application of isothermal nucleic acid amplification technology. With a team of PhD and Master's level talents in biotechnology, we specialize in RPA-based rapid detection solutions.

Our technical advantages include independent raw material production, proprietary lyophilized microsphere technology, cost-effective manufacturing, and rigorous quality control systems. We are committed to building precise, convenient and accessible bio-detection products.
